About

In this site you can watching video (movie's fragments) and improve your foregn language skills.

Just setup your native lang and find any video on target lang (The primary video's lang is pointed at []).

How does it work?

If you have watched and didn't understand, try to seek video back and subtitles on primary lang will be shown.

If you still have no idea, seek video back again and you will see subs on your native lang.

Good luck!
